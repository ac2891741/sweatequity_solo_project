Cardiovascular exercise is an important part of a healthy lifestyle, and can be a great way to stay active. Regular cardiovascular exercise has been shown to reduce the risk of high blood pressure, heart attack and stroke. It can also help with weight management and improve energy levels.

Strength training is a type of physical exercise that focuses on building strength, muscle mass and endurance. The goal of strength training is to challenge the muscles through resistance, causing them to adapt and become stronger. Strength training can be beneficial for people of all ages.
